About Formwhile

This is a space where you can explore, experiment, and practice art with few distractions.

For what these tools lack in niceties, they are made up for in direction. Since many choices about set-up are made for you, you are at liberty to use the available tools to their fullest. Your task is not to choose, but to simply make use of what you have- an invigorating, technical challenge.

Rationale

This site has features which may inconvenience visitors. Some of these are inevitably mistakes, but some are intentional.

The small drawing canvas size has two purposes: first, it reduces file size; second, it is emulating the "thumbnail" technique. The thumbnail technique is where artists will draw very small initial sketches, then magnify them into the final drawing. The idea is that the artist is focusing only on the big picture when drawing so small- they only capture the essential shapes of the scene, which make it recognizable even from a distance. In Formwhile, you are effectively only performing that first step, focusing not on minutiae but on what the big idea you want to convey is.
